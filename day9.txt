
1.calculate total value of each product adding tax of 12 percent
mysql> select name,price,price+(price*0.12)as price_with_tax from products;

2. Calculate discounted price  by subtracting price with 5 rs
mysql> select name,price,(price-5) as Discounted_price from products;

3. Total inventory value for each product (multiplication of price and tax)
mysql> select name,price,(price*qty) as  TOTAL_INVENTORY_VALUE from products;
